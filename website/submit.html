Submit Your Model

Contributions are welcome! Follow these steps to submit your Small Language Model for evaluation.

1

Prepare your YAML

Create a submission file containing your model metadata. Ensure the model is publicly accessible on Hugging Face.

model:
  name: "YourModelName-1.1B"
  family: "Llama"
  hf_repo: "username/repo"
  parameters: "1.1B"
  architecture: "LlamaForCausalLM"
  license: "Apache-2.0"
  categories: ["reasoning", "language", "tool_use", "safety"]
2

Open an Issue

Go to our GitHub repository and open a new issue using the "Model Submission" template. Paste your YAML in the description.

3

Automated Evaluation

Our Community Runners will automatically pick up your submission, run the benchmarks on CPU, and update the leaderboard within 24 hours.
